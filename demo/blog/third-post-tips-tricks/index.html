Power User Tips & Tricks

Maya Patel · February 28, 2026 · 3 min read · Tips

Here are some quick tips for getting more out of Acme:

  • Use Cmd+K to open the command palette
  • Press G then W to jump to any workflow
  • Star frequently used workflows for quick access
  • Use the bulk-action toolbar to update multiple records at once
  • Enable "Focus Mode" in settings to hide the sidebar
  • Use variables in workflow names for dynamic labeling
  • Set up Slack notifications for critical workflow failures
