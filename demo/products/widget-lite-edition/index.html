Overview

Widget Lite is the fastest way to get started with automation. It has everything a small team or solo operator needs to automate their core workflows — no enterprise contract, no complexity, no minimum seat count.

Everything in Widget Lite is designed for simplicity: guided setup wizards, starter templates for the most common use cases, and a clean interface that doesn't overwhelm first-time automation builders.

What's Included

  • Up to 3 team members
  • 5 active workflows
  • 1,000 automation runs per month
  • 50+ integrations including Slack, Gmail, Notion, Google Sheets
  • Pre-built starter templates
  • Real-time run history (last 30 days)
  • Email support

Who Is It For?

Widget Lite is ideal for:

  • Solo founders and freelancers who want to automate admin work
  • Small teams (under 5 people) who are new to workflow automation
  • Side projects and early-stage startups on tight budgets
  • Teams evaluating Acme before committing to a paid plan

Upgrading is easy. When you outgrow Widget Lite, you can upgrade to Widget Pro in one click. All your workflows carry over — no migration required.
